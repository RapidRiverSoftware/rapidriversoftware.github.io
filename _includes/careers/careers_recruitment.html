Our recruitment process

Every candidate is unique, and we like to treat them as such. But just for peace of mind, this is what a typical process would look like.

1. First interview

During this step, we will get to know a little bit more about you and whether you're particular skills are suitable for the roles we're hiring for.

2. Pairing exercise

If the initial interview goes well, we will move onto a technical task that is to be done with one of our senior developers.

3. Code review and feedback

After the pair-programming exercise, your code will be reviewed and feedback will be provided if requested.

4. Reference checks

We will speak to a couple of previous managers to see what it would be like working with you.

5. Offer

If all the other steps have been satisfactorily checked-off, we will contact you with a job offer.
