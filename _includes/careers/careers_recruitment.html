Our recruitment process

Every candidate is unique, and we like to treat them as such. But to maximize the probability of a good fit into our team, this is what our process typically looks like.

1. Résumé Screening

We'll look at your résumé and other professional profiles (e.g. LinkedIn, Github) to get an inital feel for fitness with the position.

2. Technical Test

We'll send you a set of questions to complete in your own time. These questions test your technical knowledge and written communication skills.

3. First Interview

A single interviewer will talk with you in broad terms to get a much better understanding of what you're looking for next in your career.

4. Pairing Exercise

You'll be tasked with a technical task to be done together with two of our senior developers over a two-hour period.

5. Reference Checks

We'll speak to a couple of previous managers to see what it would be like working with you.

6. Offer

If you reach this stage, we'd love to work with you! We'll make you an official offer to join us.
