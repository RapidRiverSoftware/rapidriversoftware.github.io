About Rapid River

A global team that get things done

Rapid River Software is a worldwide group of full-stack developers with skills ranging from front-end to operations automation, and everything in between. We have more than four decades of experience writing software for the Internet. Whether designing new systems, or improving existing ones, we have the skills to hit the ground running. As developers, we understand that getting things done is paramount. We don’t waste your time with salespeople, account managers and other dead weight who can’t immediately move your project forward. When you talk to us, you’re always talking to a developer – one who can solve your problem or get you the answers you need. But don’t take our word for it; see what our customers have to say about working with us!

How we can help you

Rapid River's diverse team of engineers can help you resolve any problem

Rapid River Software developers augment existing teams to increase development bandwidth and provide expert technical guidance in a cost-effective manner. We work within groups ranging in size from one to 100 to fill in skills gaps, strengthen designs, and keep product development moving at a rapid pace. As native or fluent English speakers, we use collaboration tools such as PivotalTracker, JIRA, Slack, HipChat, and others to ensure clear communication and easy-to-follow development history.

Expertise with remote collaboration means that Rapid River Software developers can (and do) live anywhere in the world: Boston, Malaysia, and even Siberia! Our developers’ lower cost of living, combined with our lack of sales overhead, allows companies to add high-quality development resources at a fraction of the cost seen in their local, competitive hiring markets.
